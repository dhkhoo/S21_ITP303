Our Team

our team of cats lovers

Jade Coleman

Co-Founder

Cat lover and foodie to the end, Jade decided to create Kitty Kafe as a place for her to take in and give lives to strays cats, all of which deserve a home and a place which care for them.

Fletcher Medina

Co-Founder

When Jade came up to Fletcher about the idea of a Cat Cafe, he was immediately in. Together, they built the cafe which we see today from the ground up and made a home for all the cats to live and enjoy.

Nadia Nelson

Barista

For Nadia, making drinks has been one of her passions for years. Being able to support such an amazing place and being able to care for so many cats, has been a dream job she never though of having.
